1. Introduction

Crisis Containment Service CriC is a Web platform which allows managing situations of crisis (for instance: earthquakes, floods, fire, tsunamis) by authorities or different organisations.

The application uses Google Crisis Map and services like Google Person Finder, in order for the system automatically provide information used for finding a missing person and regarding eventual shelters and saving routes in cases of emergency.

The website will provide users with the ability to check again the past events in their area and if they were solved. Last but not least, users will be presented a variety of articles regarding how to help organisations, what to do in a crisis, first-aid, a page where world-wide current events are being shown and also a page where other organisations in partnership with CriC can be found.

3. What Our Website Offers

Through Crisis Containment Service, we want to provide anyone that enters our website with the essential information regarding how to help somebody and what is happening around the world.

This is why we provide users with breaking news and articles. You do not need to log in or have an account in order to access this information.

3.1 News

The Guide Page is where breaking news titles are being shown. In case you want more information on what happened, you can enter the News page, where you can find short texts that will lead you to other safe websites where full articles are being presented.

3.2 About

Crisis Containment Service is a web application which allows authorities or specialized organizations to manage emergency situations, like earthquakes, fires, floods, etc. You will be able to find information of interest regarding the events or the affected areas. Also, you can get notifications and alarms if you are affected directly or if you are in the neighborhood. Nevertheless, the system will provide information of interest for finding a missing person or related to any shelters and rescue routes.

3.3 Contact Us

We have provided a Contact footer where you can find out more information about Contact details.

Nevertheless, if you have any feedback or question for us, there is a phone number and an e-mail address where you can submit your problem.

4. Map Page

You will be able to see current events hapenning near you on the map.

4.1 See alerts nearby

You can see both current alerts and past alerts on your dashboard page that you can access from "My Dashboard" button in the upper right corner of the page.

4.2 Add a new Event

  1. In order to add a new event, you can press the button "Add event" from the Header, located in every page.
  2. You will be redirected to a form that you have to complete in which you need to describe the event.
  3. After filling in the fields, you need to press the button "Submit!". Now your event will appear on Event list in the specific section.

4.3 Find Missing Person

  1. In order to report a missing person, you need to press the button "Give informations about somebody" located on the Casualties page.
  2. To find a missing person, we need all the details that can be provided. This is why it is good to write everything you now in the form that you were redirected to.
  3. After you finished completing the form, don't forget to press "Submit!". Now the authorities will be alerted and your report will be shown in the Map Page.
